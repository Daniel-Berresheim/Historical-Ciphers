    Historical Ciphers

    In the past, encryption schemes had little in common with modern standards. Little to none access to powerful computers made it easy to only rely on obscure algorithms, which means that they often didnt even use a secret key.
    Today, those algorithms should not be used for critical applications. Still they are a good way to understand the basics of encryption.

    This tool is meant to introduce various basic substitution ciphers of the past.
